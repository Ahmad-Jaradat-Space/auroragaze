# date=2015-03-17 kp_peak=8 bz_min=-27 dst_min=-223 source=https://www.swpc.noaa.gov/news/g4-severe-geomagnetic-storm-observed-march-17-2015 event_type=storm persona=both

March 2015 St Patrick's Day storm. CME arrival on March 17. Kp reached 8
(G4 severe). Dst reached -223 nT. Bz minimum -27 nT.

Satellite impact: increased LEO drag and several reported single-event
upsets. No major losses publicly reported. HF communications were degraded
across the Pacific sector.

Aurora visibility: widespread southern Australian aurora. Reports from
Tasmania, Victoria, South Australia, and the southern coast of New South
Wales. New Zealand reports across both islands.

Reference frame: a useful G4 reference case in the previous solar cycle
(cycle 24, lower-amplitude than cycle 25). Comparison case for "G4 in a
weaker cycle."
