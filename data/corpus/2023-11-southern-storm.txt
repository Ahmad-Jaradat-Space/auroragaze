# date=2023-11-05 kp_peak=7 bz_min=-25 dst_min=-163 source=https://www.swpc.noaa.gov/news/severe-g4-geomagnetic-storm-watch-5-nov-2023 event_type=storm persona=both

November 2023 storm. CME arrival on November 5. Kp reached 7 briefly with
sustained Kp 6 conditions for most of the night UTC. Dst reached -163 nT.
Bz minimum -25 nT.

Satellite impact: nominal. No publicly-reported losses. Increased drag at
LEO; routine station-keeping.

Aurora visibility: a notable southern aurora event in Tasmania and Victoria.
Confirmed photographs from Hobart, Bruny Island, the Otways, and southern
South Australia. The display was visible for about three hours after local
sunset. New Zealand reports included Wellington and the South Island.

Reference frame: a clear case of "Kp 7 storm gives definite aurora across
southern Tasmania and Victoria but unlikely north of about -37°S." Calibration
point for the visibility threshold.
