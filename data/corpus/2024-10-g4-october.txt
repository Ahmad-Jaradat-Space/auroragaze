# date=2024-10-10 kp_peak=8 bz_min=-30 dst_min=-335 source=https://www.swpc.noaa.gov/news/severe-g4-geomagnetic-storm-warning-10-oct-2024 event_type=storm persona=both

October 2024 G4 storm. A series of CMEs from active region 13848 arrived at
Earth on October 10-11. Kp reached 8 (G4 severe). Dst reached -335 nT. Bz
minimum -30 nT. Solar wind speed peaked above 750 km/s.

Satellite impact: multiple LEO operators reported drag-related station
keeping; SpaceX Starlink fleet operations adjusted. No publicly-reported
satellite losses. GPS positioning errors reported up to several metres.

Aurora visibility: widely visible across southern Australia, New Zealand,
and unusually as far north as central Queensland and central New South
Wales. The auroraaustralis.org.au gallery for this event has confirmed
photographs from Brisbane, Sydney, and Perth.

Reference frame: a recent strong G4 event useful as a calibration point
for May 2024-style impacts at Kp 8 conditions.
