March 2025 G3 storm. Solar cycle 25 ramp continues. CME arrival
March 23. Kp peaked 7, sustained Kp 6 for ~6 hours. Dst -145 nT.
Bz minimum -22 nT.

Satellite impact: nominal LEO drag increase reported (~15-25% at
400 km); minor station-keeping reported by major operators. No
publicly reported losses.

Aurora visibility: confirmed observations across southern Tasmania
and parts of southern Victoria; New Zealand reports across both
islands.

Reference frame: a routine Kp 7 cycle-25 event. Useful as a "modern
G3" calibration point distinct from the more dramatic May 2024 case.
