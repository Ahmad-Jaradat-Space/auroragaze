South Australia aurora observations during the May 2024 Gannon storm.
Confirmed photographs from Adelaide (-34.93°S), the Adelaide Hills, the
Fleurieu Peninsula, Kangaroo Island, and as far north as the Flinders
Ranges. Reports also from Whyalla and Port Augusta.

Time window: 10:00 to 14:00 UTC. Local time 19:30 to 23:30 ACST.
Visibility quality: overhead arcs from dark sites south of Adelaide;
horizon glow with structure from the city itself.

Cloud cover: largely clear across most of the state. Moon: waxing crescent,
low impact.

Reference frame: confirms aurora overhead at -34.9°S during Kp 9 conditions.
A useful southern-state point for calibration in addition to the Tasmania
and Victoria reference cases.
