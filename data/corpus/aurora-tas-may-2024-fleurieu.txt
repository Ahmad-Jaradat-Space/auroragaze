# date=2024-05-12 location=Fleurieu Peninsula, SA kp_at_observation=8 source=https://auroraaustralis.org.au/2024-05-12-fleurieu/ event_type=aurora_observation persona=aurora

Fleurieu Peninsula aurora event, May 12 2024 (second night of the
Gannon storm). Confirmed photographs from Cape Jervis, Yankalilla,
Victor Harbor, Goolwa, and Hindmarsh Valley. Time window 11:00 to
14:00 UTC. Local 20:30 to 23:30 ACST.

The display included naked-eye pillars and arcs to the south, with
strong red-pink colour and overhead structure from darker sites
toward Cape Jervis. Visibility was rated as "exceptional" by local
observers — comparable to Hobart's typical Kp 8 displays.

Cloud cover was largely clear; moonset before peak.

Reference frame: a strong calibration point at -35.5°S during a Kp 8
sustained storm. Useful comparison case alongside Tasmanian and
Victorian reports for the same event.
